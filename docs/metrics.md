# Metrics Reference

## BMI (Body Mass Index)
BMI is calculated as weight (kg) divided by height (mÂ²).
It is commonly used to categorize individuals as underweight,
normal weight, overweight, or obese.

## BMI Change
BMI change represents the difference between follow-up BMI and
baseline BMI. A negative value indicates weight loss.

## Blood Pressure
Systolic blood pressure measures pressure during heartbeats.
Diastolic pressure measures pressure between beats.

Smoking status is known to influence blood pressure levels.
